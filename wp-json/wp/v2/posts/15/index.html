Burleigh Brewing have introduced three new lagers to their core range and a new-look as they seek to highlight a \u201cslow brewed\u201d approach to their beers.<\/strong><\/p>\n

As well as new designs across their range, Burleigh\u2019s brand reposition sees them add a mid-strength version of their pioneering Bighead zero carb lager \u2013 the Bighead Mid (3% ABV) \u2013 as well as 4.2% and 3% versions of the Slow Brewed Lager.<\/p>\n

CEO Peta Fielding, who founded the brewery on the Gold Coast with husband and master brewer Brennan in 2006, said in a statement the changes were an \u201cevolution of the brand\u2019s premium positioning\u201d.<\/p>\n

\u201cWe\u2019ve re-positioned our brand to shine a light on our slow brewing ethos.\u201d Peta said. \u201cWe like to take our time to do things right, and to us, beer done right means extra time in the tanks.<\/p>\n

\u201cOnly the natural passing of time ferments and ages our beer. No shit, no shortcuts!\u201d<\/p>\n

Burleigh said all three new lagers are available in 330ml bottles, 375ml cans and 50L kegs.<\/p>\n
