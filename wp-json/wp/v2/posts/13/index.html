Bottleshop Blackhearts & Sparrows has teamed up with the Pink Boots Society of Australia and Sydney\u2019s Philter Brewing on the limited release hazy pale ale Juicy that\u2019s been brewed to drive awareness and raise funds for empowering women in the beer industry.<\/strong><\/p>\n

With $18 from every case and $3 from each four-pack sold going directly to the Pink Boots Society of Australia, Juicy\u2019s brew day saw Blackhearts & Sparrows\u2019 beer buyer Cherry Murphy join Pink Boots\u2019 NSW members at Philter\u2019s Marrickville brewery (pictured above).<\/p>\n

Sitting right at the heart of the beer is Yakima Chief Hops\u2019 Pink Boots 2022 hop blend, made up of Loral, Eukanot and HBC 586 hops. Mosaic and Citra were also added with just a touch of Sabro.<\/p>\n

The limited-edition Juicy is available exclusively through Blackhearts & Sparrows stores and is available for purchase across Victoria, Canberra, Hobart and online.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
